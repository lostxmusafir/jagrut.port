Agents that act

Dashboards show you what happened. Aura agents watch what is happening, reason about risk, and run the playbooks you have already approved—before a human opens a laptop.

Specialized operators

Each agent is scoped to a domain: fraud, reliability, capacity, customer health, or your own runbooks. They share context through the same ledger as your analytics, so they never argue with the numbers.

Memory and refinement

Incidents and resolutions are retained as structured memory. Over time, agents narrow false positives and suggest policy updates—always with human-readable rationale for audit.

Hooks into your stack

Webhooks, APIs, Slack, PagerDuty, and Kubernetes integrations let an agent escalate or remediate only when you want it to. Nothing runs as a black box.
