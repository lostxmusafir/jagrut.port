Zero-latency context

Aura treats your operational data as a single, living system—not a pile of disconnected warehouses and batch jobs. This page is the long-form story behind the pipeline you glimpsed on the home experience.

Streaming ledger

Every change from Kafka, Postgres, object storage, and custom sources lands in an immutable event ledger. Downstream models and agents read the same truth; there is no “reporting copy” that falls behind production.

Compilers optimize hot paths so queries that used to take minutes return in milliseconds, without you hand-tuning clusters every quarter.

Resilience by default

Multi-region replication, automatic failover, and continuous verification mean your intelligence layer keeps running when a region or provider hiccups. The SLA is a product feature, not a sales footnote.

Compliance-ready

Encryption in transit and at rest, fine-grained roles, and tamper-evident audit trails are built into the core—not bolted on as a separate “governance SKU.”
