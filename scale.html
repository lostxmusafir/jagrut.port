Engineered for scale

High-frequency trading, global logistics, and regulated healthcare all share one requirement: the system must stay fast and correct when volume spikes. Aura is designed for that tail, not just the demo.

Event volume

The platform ingests and serves billions of events per day across tenants. Sharding and autoscaling are automatic; you define SLOs, not instance counts.

Latency

Predictive scheduling warms compute before predictable peaks. Cold-query surprises are treated as defects, not “how databases work.”

Always on

Planned maintenance windows are eliminated through rolling upgrades and dual-write cutovers. Your operators keep sleep; the control plane does not.
